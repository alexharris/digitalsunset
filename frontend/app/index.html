*Catch a sunset, put it in your pocket*

We all know that pictures of the sunset suck. What's better than a picture? The real sunset, duh. But second to that might be the precise digital gradient you can make with Digital Sunset. Recreate the magic magic of a sunset on your dumb smartphone.


Sunsets are ephemeral, but SVGs are forever.

Digital Sunset is mostly for your phone.

Try it there.

Put the biggest most impossible thing into a little digital square.

Digital Sunset is an app project by Alex Harris and Nicole Lavelle. It is simultaneously very important and totally useless.

They are excited to see what you do with it. Tag your things with the hashtag #digitalsunsetapp so we can peep your gradients.
